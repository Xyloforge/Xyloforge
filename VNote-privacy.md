# Privacy Policy Template for Your Note App

**Effective Date:** 2026-01-01

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how VNote (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We only collect the information necessary to provide the app features:

* **Google Account Information**: Required to sync notes with Google Drive.
* **Notes and attachments**: The content you create in the app, stored in your Google Drive.

> We do **not** collect personal data like your email, contacts, or files outside the app unless you explicitly use our sync feature.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

* **Google Drive Sync**: To save and retrieve your notes across devices.
* **App functionality**: To provide a smooth note-taking experience.

> We **do not sell or share** your information with third parties.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

* We use **Google Drive API** to store your notes in your Google account.
* Please review Google’s Privacy Policy here: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

---

## 4. Security

* Your notes are stored in **your Google Drive account**, secured by Google.
* We do **not store any notes on our servers**.

---

## 5. Children’s Privacy

* Our app is **not intended for children under 13**.
* We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## 6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:

nrt-app-support@googlegroups.com
